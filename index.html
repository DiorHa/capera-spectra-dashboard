🚀 Spectra Fit From Capera's Perspective

Spectra positions itself as a specialized nearshore extension platform for the DACH real estate and PropTech ecosystem, not a generic outsourcing provider.

Identity 1
Specialized Operator
Domain-focused real-estate/PropTech operator, not a generic labor marketplace.
Identity 2
Extension Model
Dedicated long-term teams integrated into client operations while preserving governance and quality.
Identity 3
Real-Estate Native
PropTech-native support where value compounds through industry specialization.

Why Kosovo Works

Kosovo strengthens feasibility for multilingual operations, accounting execution, onboarding services, and selected technical delivery. The core proposition is governed, cost-rational extension teams for a specialized DACH real-estate niche.

Backed By Modium Holding

Spectra states it is backed by Modium's 18 years of expertise and 150+ architectural projects, carrying forward a precision-oriented operating culture.

I. Managed Team Infrastructure

Employer-of-record backbone for recruitment, compliance, payroll, and HR operations. Why it matters: faster activation without early foreign-employment setup friction.

II. Platform Onboarding, Master Data & Data Operations

Operational readiness for onboarding and migration, not basic data entry. Why it matters: reduces errors that impact reporting, invoicing, and platform trust.

III. Accounting & Bookkeeping Support

Recurring bookkeeping, reconciliations, VAT processing, and closings. Why it matters: absorbs repeatable, quality-sensitive load that constrains local teams.

IV. Analytics, Development & Workflow Intelligence

Long-term, industry-specific expansion layer. Why it matters: becomes credible after repeated workflow knowledge and trusted delivery are established.

⚠ Language & Compliance Requirements
German language: Required for Band A (debtor correspondence/dunning) and Band C (tenant-facing). Recommended B2+ for Band A and C1 for Band C. Band B can operate in English with German B1+ for system labels and documentation.
GDPR: All team members must complete GDPR processor training before go-live. Spectra operates as data processor under Art. 28 GDPR. Role-based access controls and audit logging mandatory across all three prioritized bands.
